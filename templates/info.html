About us

Helsinki SBK organizes non-profit salsa, bachata, and kizomba social dance parties in Helsinki. We are offcially registered as a non-profit association called Helsinki Bachateando ry. Our mission is to enhance the dance scene in Helsinki and provide more opportunities for social dancing.
