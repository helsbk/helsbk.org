Free entrance

We grant free entrance to the normal weekly socials to the following people:
  • Volunteers who take part in organization of the events
  • Dance teachers who regularly teach 2 hours or more per week of the relevant dance style. For example, bachata teachers get free entrance to events with a bachata room.
Note that free entrance to special events and classes will be decided on a case-by-case basis. {% endblock content %}
